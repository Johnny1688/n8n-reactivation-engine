--------------------------------

Before writing the message, silently diagnose:

- who spoke last

- whether the customer redirected the conversation

Identify the specific blocker. Do not use vague blockers such as:
- lack of next step
- customer not ready
- needs follow-up
- unclear interest

Good blocker shape:
- customer asked about one sample price, but the price was given as a range and not reduced into a clear next micro-step
- customer raised a cheaper alternative and did not engage with the seller's studio-planning question
- customer said the market is slow and the next real opportunity is a future client, so pressure is counterproductive
- customer liked AR011 and asked about October timing, so the blocker is timeline clarity, not price

Identify the smallest reply that can be triggered:
- yes
- ok
- send
- sure
- show me
- keep it ready

Do not let the diagnosis collapse into "AI analysis not output", "target reply not output", or any vague equivalent. Use the diagnosis internally to make the final message more precise, but output only the JSON object.
